[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Acoustic guitar strums softly, intimate warmth]
I’m pouring out another late-night cup
Just to watch the moon keep shining up
There’s a quiet kind of heaven in this room
When all I hear is your breathing cut through the gloom

[Verse 1]
[Gentle fingerpicking begins, adding a tender rhythm]
The city outside is slowing its spin
But in here, it’s just the blankets and your skin
I’ve memorized the way your shoulders fall
And how your shadows dance across my wall
I trace the lines of the road we’ve driven down
Right here in the silence of this sleepy town

[Pre-Chorus]
[Subtle bass slide, building anticipation]
I don’t want to wake you, but I don’t want to sleep
There’s a memory in the making that I’ve got to keep

[Chorus]
[Smooth, full acoustic band kicks in with a warm, steady groove]
Yeah, I’m just sitting here watching you sleep
Getting lost in a rhythm that runs so deep
With the porch light glowing on your pretty face
I finally found my forever resting place
It’s a simple kind of perfect I want to keep
Yeah, I’m just sitting here watching you sleep

[Post-Chorus]
[Acoustic guitar riff, melodic and soft]
Watching you sleep, yeah
The best kind of secret for me to keep

[Verse 2]
[Drums pull back slightly, keeping it close and intimate]
I remember when we met beneath the neon signs
Now it’s just your silhouette that blurs the lines
You pull the sheets a little closer to your chin
And I’m falling for you all over again
There’s a fire in the hearth, but you’re the flame
I could spend a lifetime whispering your name

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Building warmth, soft electric guitar swells]
I don’t want to wake you, but I don’t want to sleep
There’s a feeling in this moment that runs so deep

[Chorus]
[Full, smooth country-pop rhythm, emotionally soaring but restrained]
Yeah, I’m just sitting here watching you sleep
Getting lost in a rhythm that runs so deep
With the porch light glowing on your pretty face
I finally found my forever resting place
It’s a simple kind of perfect I want to keep
Yeah, I’m just sitting here watching you sleep

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Warm acoustic strumming repeats the hook]
Watching you sleep, yeah
The best kind of secret for me to keep

[Bridge]
[Music strips down to just piano and soft acoustic guitar, a vulnerable pause]
You’re the dream that Saeed never wants to break
I’d stay awake forever just for your sake

[Spoken]
[Music halts, completely silent, a quiet, breathy confession]
I could watch you for a lifetime.
Just don’t open your eyes yet.

[Final Chorus]
[The full band swells, warm and triumphant, a soulful, atmospheric finish]
Yeah, I’m just sitting here watching you sleep
Getting lost in a rhythm that runs so deep
With the porch light glowing on your pretty face
I finally found my forever resting place
It’s a simple kind of perfect I want to keep
Yeah, I’m just sitting here watching you sleep
